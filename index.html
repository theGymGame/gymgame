THE GYM GAME

The Rules

  • You must go to the gym four days a week, except for certain cirmcumstances (see below)
  • Gym sessions must last at least 40 minutes
  • You have 2 strikes available when you start the game and one make up per week
  • If you miss a day, you must make it up WITHIN the week in order for it to not count as a strike
  • The person(s) to hit 2 strikes first must buy the other game members MILKSHAKES FROM COOKOUT
  • Everytime a new player joins the game, the every player resets back to 0 strikes

Exceptions

  • If you're deathly ill (heartbreaks don't count) or there is family emergency, it is a free skip that you don't need to make up
  • If you're Nishta, you only work out three times a week due to Salsa
  • If you're traveling for 1 week or under, they may also count as a free skip that doesn't need to be made up
